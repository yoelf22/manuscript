## Book Organization Summary

The book follows a structured approach to smart tangible product development:

1. **Diagnosing Challenges** - Understanding unique hardware difficulties
2. **Validation Strategy** - Timing and cycles for physical product validation  
3. **Marketing & Positioning** - Creating compelling product narratives
4. **Digital Integration** - Leveraging software for hardware advantage
5. **Business Models** - Sustainable revenue and AI integration
