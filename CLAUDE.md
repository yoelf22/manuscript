# CLAUDE.md - Smart Tangibles Book Project

## Project Overview

**Smart Tangibles: Strategic Product Management for Connected Hardware**

A comprehensive business book guiding hardware entrepreneurs and product managers through the transformation of physical products into software-augmented platforms. The book addresses the unique challenges of hardware development in an increasingly digital world, providing frameworks for validation, competitive positioning, and business model innovation.

## Target Audience
- Product managers transitioning to hardware companies
- Hardware startup founders and entrepreneurs  
- Innovation leaders in established hardware companies
- Engineers bridging physical and digital product development

## Project Structure

### Book Architecture
- **6 Major Arcs** covering hardware challenges through business models
- **25 Chapters** (0-24) with case studies, frameworks, and practical guidance
- **Arc introduction files** providing context for each major section
- **Extensive visual materials** including charts, diagrams, and photographs
- **Real-world case studies** (CycleBe, iRobot, Tesla, BYD, Apple, etc.)

## Key Themes

1. **Platform Absorption**: How smartphones cannibalize standalone devices
2. **Hardware Validation**: Unique challenges vs. software development
3. **Competitive Evolution**: Global manufacturing and cost arbitrage
4. **Digital Transformation**: IoT, connectivity, and edge computing
5. **Business Model Innovation**: From products to platforms and services
6. **Security & Ethics**: Privacy, cybersecurity, and social impact

## Writing Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Professional but accessible**: Business book style, not academic
- **Data-driven**: Include specific statistics and concrete examples
- **Practical focus**: Actionable frameworks over pure theory
- **Case study rich**: Real companies and products as validation

### Content Standards
- **Clear structure**: Logical progression from problem to solution
- **Visual support**: Charts and images that enhance understanding  
- **International perspective**: Global manufacturing and competition context
- **Current relevance**: Modern examples and recent data

### Chapter Quality Criteria
- **Strong opening**: Compelling hook with concrete evidence
- **Clear thesis**: One main argument per chapter
- **Supporting evidence**: Multiple data points and examples
- **Practical application**: How readers can apply insights
- **Smooth transitions**: Logical flow between sections

## Content Guidelines

### Case Studies
- **Real companies**: Avoid hypothetical examples
- **Specific data**: Include concrete numbers and timelines
- **Multiple perspectives**: Show both successes and failures
- **Lessons extracted**: Clear strategic insights for readers

### Visual Materials
- **Professional attribution**: Proper Wikimedia/source credits
- **Strategic placement**: Images that support narrative flow
- **Consistent quality**: High-resolution, relevant visuals
- **Accessibility**: Clear captions and descriptions

### Technical Accuracy
- **Current data**: Recent statistics and market information
- **Industry terminology**: Correct technical language
- **Global perspective**: International examples and competition
- **Future-oriented**: Emerging trends and technologies
